About

This workshop aims to explore multimodal approaches for household robotics. We plan to discuss how to meet challenges not only in their technological aspects but also with regard to human-robot communication (HRC) and interaction (HRI) by bringing together experts from different domains. We have invited two keynote speakers covering different aspects of household robotics in academia and industry. This will be followed by presentations of accepted submissions. The attendees will be divided into 5-8 groups depending on the number of par- ticipants. Each group will be assigned a small set of challenges related to house hold robotics and will be asked to brainstorm how to overcome these. Finally, a group discussion led by the keynote speakers is planned to integrate the results of all groups. At the end of the workshop, we will identify critical challenges that must be addressed to have robots perform household chores and broaden our understanding of other related disciplines
